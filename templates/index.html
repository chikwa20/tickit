About us

TICK.IT is a Cinema ticketing system that allows you to book a ticket to watch movies in cinema. The user of this system is the Admin and Staff. The Admin controls updating what movies this week is available and others. While the Staff is in charge in filling up the details needed from customer to book the ticket. There are just specific Malls and places that we offer like SM seaside Cebu Cinema, Gaisano Grand Minglanilla Cinema, Nustar Cebu Cinema, Cebu IL CORSO Cinema, UC(University of Cantao-an) and TOPS Cebu skydom.
